Our Animals

Our zoo is home to a wide variety of animals, including lions, tigers, bears, and monkeys. Click on the links below to learn more about each of our animals.

Lions

Lions are large cats that are native to Africa. They are known for their golden manes and powerful roar. Our lions can be found in the savanna exhibit.

Tigers

Tigers are large cats that are native to Asia. They are known for their distinctive orange and black stripes. Our tigers can be found in the tropical rainforest exhibit.

Bears

Bears are large, carnivorous mammals found in a variety of habitats around the world. Our bears can be found in the woodlands exhibit.

Monkeys

Monkeys are intelligent primates that can be found in a variety of habitats around the world. Our monkeys can be found in the tropical rainforest exhibit.
